COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING VADAKARA

Kozhikode,Kerala

The college of enginering Vadakara is an engineering college in kozhikode district of Kerala,established in 1999. The first engineering college under the Co-operative Academy of Professional Education,Thiruvanandhapuram,established by Govt.of Kerala,started functioning in June 1999. The college offers undergraduate programmes and postgraduate programmes under APJKTU.Undergraduate programmes include CE,EEE,CSE,ECE,EIE,IT,The college also offers MCA at post graduate level.
